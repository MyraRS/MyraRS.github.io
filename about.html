About this project

My school hosted a talk about pollution on Earth and how to reduce it, with a focus on Malaysia in particular. This inspired me to create a website where people could easily find and browse eco-friendly or sustainable places and products. As stated in Genesis 2:15, The Lord God took the man and put him in the garden of Eden to work it and keep it. I created the website with this scripture and the Earth's well-being in mind.
